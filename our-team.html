Partners

Rekha Chakradhar

Partner


Mrs. Rekha Chakradhar acquired her Bachelor of Arts in 1988 and Bachelor of Laws in 1992, both from the University of Bangalore and enrolled as Advocate in the Bar Council of Karnataka in 2017.
Rekha Chakradhar by aspiring and becoming a founding member of Pragma Law & Policy is telling the world that women can kickstart their professional careers at any age, by choice. After having acquired a well appreciated proficiency spanning more than 2 decades in Indian Tanjore Art, she is now contributing to the growth of our Firm by managing its clientele and treasury affairs.

 

Head - Policy

Anirudh Chakradhar

Head - Policy


Mr. Anirudh Chakradhar is a young professional holding a Bachelors degree in English Literature from Delhi University, and a Masters degree in Public Policy from the National Law School of India University, Bengaluru. Prior to Pragma, Anirudh worked for a multi-national consulting firm, and has provided advisory services to various Public Sector clients in several projects.

Anirudh’s expertise is in the research, analysis, planning, formulation and monitoring & evaluation of government & business policies. This allows him to provide insight to both the policy makers for drafting of appropriate policy as well as in providing advisory services to companies and firms which enables them to navigate the complex policy & regulatory environment while ensuring statutory compliance.

 

Associates
