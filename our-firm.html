Who We Are: Our Firm

Our Firm

Pragma Law & Policy is a boutique Law Firm headquartered in Bangalore. Our ambition is to consistently provide strategic & effective solutions in the fields of Law & Public Policy, with a Touch of Pragmatism.

Our clients - whether Corporates, Enterprises or Industries - can leverage the vast experience of our firm’s partners & mentors and seek out the capabilities of our wide network of associateships with like-minded professionals for legal solutions, consultation and advisory in niche areas of Law.

We provide advisory and research services in chosen fields to Corporates, MSMEs, educational institutions, healthcare service industries as well as government institutions & Civil Society Organizations, besides taking up social causes on behalf of citizens.



History

The endeavour to employ a pragmatic & strategic approach to devise appropriate & effective legal solutions brought our partners together. Each of our partners shared a fundamental thought that gave Pragma its current form and the common theme of it all is “Pragmatism”. This became our cornerstone at the time when the foundations of the Firm were being conceived and laid.

Our Founders bring to the table to serve our clients, an amalgam of rich and cumulative experience of over 60 years of service in large renowned Corporates as well as the Government, both focussed toward industry and consumers. Our partners, having dealt with real time commercial corporate situations in the packaged commodities & foods industry are best equipped to provide effective solutions for corporates. Such legal services & solutions are well supported with clarity about government enforcement systems, compliance check & adherence to regulation.

We also have focussed expertise in Public Policy & Development. This adds another layer of being able to develop effective policy solutions on various issues and represent the same to the government to promote a growing economy. We are also able to ensure that business and industrial enterprises can be supported with best internal policies to promote safe, inclusive, sustainable development of corporate workspaces.

This coming together of disciplines, experience and people, gave birth to Pragma Law & Policy from the summer of 2017.

While Pragma is still at its nascent stages, we are geared to accomplish a vision to display swiftness, deliver quality and demonstrate accessibility to every client. We believe, we are uniquely poised with our interdisciplinary skill sets to provide a new depth to each of our clients and their needs.



Vision

To devise strategic, effective, responsible and sustainable solutions to challenges in the fields of Law & Policy, with a Touch of Pragmatism.



Mission

  • To display swiftness, deliver quality and demonstrate accessibility to every client

  • To contribute to collaborative nation-building through growth of industry, economy & employment



Guiding Principles

Pragmatic Stratagem

We value and believe that all strategies should be grounded in pragmatism for effective services and measurement of client-desired results as per rule of law.

Team

Our team works as one cohesive unit. Each Partner, Employee and Associate all being part of a single team but each one bringing in their own skills, experience and knowledge to the team, ensures world class service quality in results.

Client Focus

We place the client at the centre of all our interactions, and maintain accessibility & transparency. We understand & tailor our approach based on client needs, and show agility to consistently & swiftly display quality in deliverables, well exceeding client expectations.

Quality Oriented

We consistently strive to provide the highest quality in our service offerings to clients, while continuously working to improve and be better than our previous best.
